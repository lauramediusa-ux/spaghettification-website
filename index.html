03 — Who We Are

About

We make digital things look like they mean it.

Spaghettification is a Berlin-based design and development studio built for brands that refuse to be ordinary. We work with digital startups and fintech companies who understand that design isn't decoration — it's infrastructure. Every pixel deliberate. Every interaction earned.

Founded in 2026, we believe the best work sits at the edge of craft and code. We're small by choice, hands-on by nature, and relentlessly focused on the details that separate good from unforgettable.
