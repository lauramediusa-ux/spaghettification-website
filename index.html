03 — Who We Are

About

Design is the difference between being seen and being remembered.

Spaghettification is the studio of Laura Angeli, Creative Director and founder. Built in Berlin in 2026 with one conviction: that great design is never decoration — it's infrastructure. We work with digital startups and fintech companies, and bring in the right collaborators for the right projects — keeping the team lean, the thinking sharp, and the work uncompromising.
